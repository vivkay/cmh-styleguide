Introduction

Our websites are globally connected platforms that showcase the extraordinary events, objects, people and stories that are presented by our world-class institutions.

Goals

We aim to serve and delight our diverse online audiences by presenting our expansive and richly varied content in an accessible, flexible and compelling way.

Principles

The CMH and CWM websites use the same framework. This website design should be informative, easy to use, and fun to explore.

Informative

The website must support a range of graphical, textual and multimedia formats, to provide a range of information that is accessible, accurate and up-to-date.

  • Easily identifiable navigational links.
  • Content filters that enable user-centered content customization.

Fun to Explore

In this new Experience Economy, users are looking for ways to engage with content. Our website will tell a rich and engaging story of our museums through:

  • Gorgeous photography.
  • Dynamic elements that respond to user interaction.
  • Fresh content that integrates live social media updates.
